Most Requested Products

lentils

Lentils are a type of legume characterized by small, oval-shaped grains and a corrugated surface. They grow in different regions of the world, and are widely used in cooking. They are considered foods rich in vegetable protein, dietary fiber, and minerals such as iron, zinc, and magnesium. They also contain group vitamins.

rice

Rice is a type of food grain that is one of the most important agricultural crops in the world. It is characterized by small, oval-shaped seeds, which are the main part of the plant. Rice is grown all over the world, and it is characterized by its high amount of carbohydrates and dietary fiber and is a good source of energy, as it contains a small amount of fats and proteins

BEANS

Beans are a type of vegetable legume, characterized by large, oval-shaped grains and a soft texture, and are available in many types. Beans are grown in most parts of the world, and are considered rich sources of vegetable protein. They also contain dietary fiber, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and magnesium.

WHITE BEANS

White beans are a type of bean characterized by oval-shaped grains and white color. It is considered a food rich in vegetable protein, dietary fiber, and minerals such as iron, zinc, and magnesium. It also contains carbohydrates and vitamins necessary for a healthy body.

POP.CORN

Popcorn popcorn is a small grain of corn that explodes when heated to produce popcorn. Popcorn has a tough outer layer called the husk and a lighter inner layer that contains moisture, starch, and oils. When the popcorn button is heated to a high temperature, the moisture inside expands until the shell bursts into the shape of the popcorn.

BASMTI RICE

Basmati rice is a type of rice that is distinguished by its long and slender grains and its light white colour. It is considered one of the most widely used types of rice around the world. It is famous for its light and delicious flavour, and is suitable for preparing a wide range of dishes.
